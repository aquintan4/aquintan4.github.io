About me

I am a Robotics Software Engineering student with a strong focus on ROS 2, C++ and Python, interested in building reliable and scalable software for autonomous robotic systems.

My main areas of interest include robot navigation, control systems, sensor integration and real-world robotics, with a strong emphasis on writing clean, maintainable and well-structured code.

I enjoy working across the full robotics software stack, from low-level logic and system architecture to simulation, testing and integration in environments such as Gazebo and Linux-based systems.

Through academic and personal projects, I continuously apply engineering principles to solve practical problems and improve my skills as a robotics software engineer.
